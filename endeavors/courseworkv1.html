    Biological Thought and Action (SHC 115)

    About
    Close

    Concerned with "historical and contemporary examples that are especially illustrative of biological thought and action and its social ramifications." Part of selective NU BioScientist Program.

    About
    Close

    Studies in Postcolonial Literature: England and it's Others (ENG 365)

    About
    Close

    Studied works to "interrogate and complicate the very notions and meanings of terms such as Englishness, Britishness, native, and foreigner. All of these works pose difficult and disturbing questions about the way we and our particular cultures deal with ourselves (as home culture) and with each other (as other, alien, or "native" cultures)." Studied "works on similar topics arising from both the metropole (the seat of the colonizer) and from the colonized (mostly from Africa, India, and Ireland)", including readings of "non-fiction about the colonial condition."
